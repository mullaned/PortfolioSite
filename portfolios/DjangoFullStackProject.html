Django Full Stack

Django Example Project

The focus of this Django project is on the backend with account management, content management and payment integration. On the front-end it is a premium news site with a pay-wall to access the content. Only users that have signed up with their payment information are able to access the content. There is a product catalogue with payment options, a contact and commenting system, a forum for user discussion and user account management.
